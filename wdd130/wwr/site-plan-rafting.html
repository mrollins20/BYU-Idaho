Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide useful information, trips, and products to anyone who is interested about white water rafting. We need this website to reach the ever-growing market on the internet and to be better able to reach or communicate with our customers beyond our local area. We hope that through these means, that we can accomplish our goal of providing a means of exciting and enjoyable entertainment outdoors! We believe that this will benefit not only ourselves, but those who enjoy white water rafting by offering information and products at a competitive rate.

Audience

Our website and business, together, plan to provide adventures and other fun experiences to families and friends of Colorado around the Glenwood Springs area; Tourists visiting the area are also welcome!

Whether you need rentals or some trip planning; We can help you! By offering rentals on all sorts of products and tools that are useful to beginning your adventure or even returning; And with personel who have rafted the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers for years, we can custom-tailor an adventure to your needs. Our promise is the perfect experience for everyone!
