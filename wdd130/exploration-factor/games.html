Want to start playing?

You can start with some of these great series! We recommend playing some of them in order, but it may be more fun to sequence break a little bit.

What we recommend:

Metroid™

As a lone bounty hunter, Samus Aran, you must navigate an alien world to discover power ups and defeat your enemies. Being one of the pioneers of the genre, Metroid achieves a sense of isolation and dread like no other game in the genre.

Castlevania™

The other pioneer to the genre, you are a vampire hunter fighting to save a besieged city from an army of otherworldly creatures controlled by Dracula.

Ori™

As Ori, last of the light spirits, you must restore balance to the forest and its inhabitants.

Dead Cells™

Dead Cells is a unique take on metroidvanias by introducing rogue-like gameplay. As you play you will find yourself repeating randomly generated levels to complete your objective.

Hollow Knight™

You are "The Knight", a traveller from outstide the kingdom of Hollow Nest. You travel to explore the vast kingdom and discover its secrets.

Grime™

GRIME takes many of the aspects of the "souls" genre's difficulty and brings it to the metroidvania genre. Gain strength by absorbing your enemies' strength and finding new weapons to fell mighty foes.
